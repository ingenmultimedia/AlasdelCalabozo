Walk, Play, Collect, and ¡Learn!

Gamify your real-world journeys with this game that takes you through dungeons and fight you against fantastic creatures to collect clues about the endemic birds of your region.

Learn, collect, and take on the final challenge together with your friends to set free... the wings of the dungeon.

Meet the captive birds in the dungeons

Identifying birds and learning about their habits will not only allow you to release them within the game, but you will also become more aware of how to interact with them when you encounter them in their natural habitat, contributing to their preservation.
